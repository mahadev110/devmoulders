Welcome to Dev Moulders

Dev Moulders was established in the 1995 for the manufacture of Plastic Injection Moulded parts. The Company specializes in Engineering Plastics and has the technical capability for processing GF Nylon, PC, ABS, Polyacetal, PPS, PBT etc.

In 2007 we diversified into manufacture of Insert Moulded Parts using Automatic Vertical Injection Moulding Machines. Using this process we manufacture Power Cords, USB and other Data Cables. We also have expertise in manufacture of Wiring Harness.

We manufacture all types of Power Cords (both 6A and 16A) with IEC - C13 socket and also various types of Grommet as per customer needs. We are an ISI certified power cord manufacturer (for 6A and 16A) and our CM/L Number is 6932382

Tailored Engineering Solutions for Every Requirement

We specialize in custom designs for plastic moulded components, power cords, and wiring harnesses, crafted to meet specific client applications.

  • Expertise in engineering-grade plastics including GF Nylon, PC, ABS, PPS, and more.
  • Customized grommets, cable ends, and socket types as per industrial specifications.
  • Dedicated design support to ensure optimal product performance and durability.

With in-house moulding and assembly capabilities, we can quickly turn around prototypes and scale up to full production efficiently.

Driven by Precision and Innovation

We continuously upgrade our machinery and processes to deliver world-class manufacturing services.

  • Equipped with advanced Vertical and Horizontal Injection Moulding Machines.
  • State-of-the-art tools for wire processing, crimping, and soldering.
  • Commitment to adopting automation for better efficiency and consistency.

From mould development to final product, our facilities are built to support continuous improvement and technological growth.

Ensuring Excellence with Every Component

Quality is embedded into our workflow, starting from raw material testing to final product validation.

  • In-house lab with ISI-certified testing facilities for power cords.
  • Equipment for CR value testing, high voltage and continuity checks.
  • Regular calibration of instruments like micrometers, gauges, and muffle furnaces.

Our stringent quality control processes ensure that each batch meets regulatory standards and customer satisfaction.
